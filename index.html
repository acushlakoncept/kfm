About Us

The company Kings Flour Mill ltd ,Onna L G A, Akwa Ibom State was established as a private company on the 13th June,2017,and approved by CAC with RC: 1418633.

The company was established for the production of high quality wheat flour and agro allied products. KFM is equipped with the efficient and trained staff The mill has a production capacity of 500 metric tons per day .
